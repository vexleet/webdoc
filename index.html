Accommodation

Finding a room in a foreign country can be overwhelming. But don’t worry, Denmark has your back. There is A housing guarantee for every student who is enrolled in a higher education. Awesome right?

Check www.studenthousingaarhus.com.

RIDE WITH ME

Public transportation is my absolute go-to here in Aarhus. It is relatively cheap, always on time and very clean. Plus, we save mama earth from pollution. You are already convinced right?

To get an idea, let’s say you live close to the Academy in Viby, but you love to spend your free time downtown. All you have to do is get a “transit ticket”, select “adult”, “1-2 zones” It will cost you 384kr. FOR THE WHOLE MONTH.

Check midttrafik for more info.
